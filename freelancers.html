    RightGigs is an independent e-commerce platform created by three spirited entrepreneurs that allows employment for artists, designers and freelancers from various firms, businesses and individual employers.



    Designers can exhibit their work and companies can ask for commissions and negotiate with the artist off-site. Through various distinct search filters, employers can narrow the copious proportion of artists down to a few, specific to their requirements.



    The website will take a fixed percentage per commission, but signing up and registering will be free. All artists and companies will be licensed and authorized and will have some legal identification to submit while registering.
